Development of AI-Based Android App for Social Awareness for COVID-19

Welcome to the forefront of technology in the fight against COVID-19. Our project, "Development of AI-Based Android App for Social Awareness for COVID-19," harnesses the power of cutting-edge technologies to create a holistic solution for social awareness and healthcare during these challenging times.

Our project leverages an array of technologies to deliver a seamless experience:


Portal (Web Application)

  • ● ASP.NET/.NET CORE for robust backend

  • ● Angular 10 and JavaScript for a responsive user interface

  • ● Python NLTK and TensorFlow for advanced natural language processing and AI capabilities


Mobile Development

  • ● Android Native App Development ensures native performance and reliability

  • ● IONIC and CAPACITOR for cross-platform mobile app development

  • ● WebAPI for seamless data communication

  • ● Angular, Python NLTK, and TensorFlow integrated for enhanced functionalities
